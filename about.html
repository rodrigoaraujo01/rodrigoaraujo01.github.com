Hello there.

My name is Rodrigo Araujo and I'm from Brazil.

Creating a blog has been a long time desire.

I've already tried twice (linil and o fantastico mundo geek). The first, was a group attempt at my old job and it was mildly successfull as it still gets constant 150+ page views everyday mostly originated on Stackoverflow. The second one, written only in portuguese, has never hit the jackpot and its most popular page (a post about the mytical, at the time, Google Drive) shows only 264 views.

Sometime ago, I read in Hacker News about static blogs being developed in Ruby and being hosted on GitHub using a library known as Jekyll. As I don't know Ruby and though I do plan to learn it someday (far in the future), I already know Python.

Searching the Internet, I found Hyde which fulfills the same purpose as Jekyll, but is written in Python.

Before settling for Hyde, I tried other options like Pelican or Flask but they were either too complex for my needs or had messy workflows.

So, here I am.

I've graduated as a Computer Engineer but, today, I work as a Petroleum Engineer. Therefore, maintaining a blog is a way to hone my coding skills as they're not much used on the day job.

The other reason is to provide a home to my pet projects as they've been living in the wild for a while now.

Feel free to contact me.
